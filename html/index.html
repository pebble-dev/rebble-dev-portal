Account Recovery

Before the Pebble shutdown in 2018, we asked you to link your Pebble account. If you didn't manage to do that before Pebble shut down, or if you have become a developer using the old rebble.io/submit tool, your Rebble account might not be linked to the correct developer account.

Things you will need
  • Your original developer ID
  • Proof of ownership
  • Your Rebble account login ID

If you don't know your original developer ID, follow this guide.
If you became a developer using the rebble.io/submit tool, then the proof of your ownership is your email account.
